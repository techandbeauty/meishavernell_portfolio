Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit meishavernell.com and interact with our services.

Information We Collect

When you sign up for ASVAB study resources or live sessions, we collect:

  • Full Name - So we can address you personally
  • Email Address - To send you resources and updates
  • Phone Number (optional) - If you'd like direct contact
  • Military Branch (optional) - To tailor resources to your needs
  • Target Test Date (optional) - To provide timely support

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Send you ASVAB study resources and materials
  • Invite you to live study sessions
  • Keep you updated on new resources and opportunities
  • Recognize you when you return to sign up again

  • Improve our services based on user feedback

How Long We Keep Your Information

We keep your information indefinitely so that:

  • You can access resources anytime without re-entering your information
  • We can send you study updates and new materials when they're available
  • You maintain your subscription to our communications
  • You're recognized when you return months or years later
  • We can maintain a record of your engagement with us

This approach is standard in the industry and matches how major platforms like Google, Facebook, and other services handle user data.

Your Rights and Choices

You have full control over your information:

1. Unsubscribe from Emails

You can unsubscribe from our mailing list anytime by:

  • Replying to any email with "unsubscribe"
  • Emailing us at: officeof.meishavernell@gmail.com
  • Including "unsubscribe" in your message

When you unsubscribe, we'll stop sending you emails but keep your contact information in case you want to resubscribe later.

2. Access Your Data

You can request to see what information we have stored about you. Email us at: officeof.meishavernell@gmail.com

3. Update Your Information

If your name, email, or other details change, let us know and we'll update your profile.

4. Delete Your Data (Right to Be Forgotten)

You can request complete deletion of your information at any time by emailing:

officeof.meishavernell@gmail.com

Please include "Delete my data" in your subject line. We will permanently remove all your information from our systems within 30 days and confirm the deletion via email.

How We Protect Your Data

We take security seriously:

  • HTTPS Encryption: All data transmitted to our servers is encrypted using industry-standard HTTPS
  • Database Encryption: All data stored in our database is encrypted at rest
  • Firebase Security: Your data is stored in Firebase, which provides enterprise-grade security
  • Access Control: Only I (the site owner) can access your personal information
  • Regular Audits: I review security settings monthly
  • No Sharing: Your information is never shared with third parties, sold, or used for marketing

To exercise any of these rights, email: officeof.meishavernell@gmail.com

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)
